Group Reflection

This is an online travel software. When it is not convenient for people to go out, such as a rainstorm, sun exposure, special period (COVID-19), people can use this app to simulate travel, travel, and make friends and chat. It make users travel at home through game way . The app uses a 2D map and panoramic map technology, and the user controls a villain to move in the application. And it's moving at real speed. Chat with people on the same screen and even chat in the same area.
